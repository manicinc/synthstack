## Getting Your Referral Code

### Step 1: Sign In

First, make sure you're signed in to your SynthStack account. If you don't have one yet, [create a free account](https://synthstack.app/auth/register).

### Step 2: Visit the Referral Page

Navigate to the **Referral** page from your dashboard menu, or go directly to [synthstack.app/referral](https://synthstack.app/referral).

### Step 3: Get Your Unique Code

Your personal referral code is automatically generated when you first visit the referral page. You'll see:

- **Your Code**: A unique alphanumeric code (e.g., `JOHN123`)
- **Your Link**: A shareable URL like `https://synthstack.app/ref/JOHN123`

> **Tip**: Your code is permanent and tied to your account. You can share it anywhere!

## Claiming Your Rewards

### How to Claim

1. Go to your **Referral Dashboard**
2. Look for the **Rewards** section
3. Click **Claim** on any unlocked reward
4. Follow the instructions based on reward type

### Reward Types

#### Discount Codes
- Automatically generated when claimed
- Can be used at checkout
- May have usage limits or expiration dates

#### Credits
- Added directly to your account balance
- Use for AI generations and workflow executions
- Never expire

#### Free Months
- Applied to your subscription
- Starts from your next billing cycle
- Stacks with existing subscription

#### Tier Upgrades
- Immediately upgrades your account
- Duration depends on the reward

### Where to Find Your Discount Codes

Claimed discount codes appear in:
- The **Rewards** section of your referral dashboard
- Your account settings under **Discount Codes**
- Email confirmation after claiming

## Tips for Success

### 1. Be Authentic

Share your genuine experience with SynthStack. People respond better to authentic recommendations than generic promotions.

**Good**: "I've been using SynthStack for my AI workflows and it's saved me hours every week. Here's my referral link if you want to try it!"

**Less effective**: "Sign up with my link for a discount!"

### 2. Target the Right Audience

Focus on people who would genuinely benefit from SynthStack:
- Developers building AI applications
- Agencies managing multiple clients
- Content creators using AI tools
- Businesses automating workflows

### 3. Provide Value First

Before sharing your link:
- Answer questions about SynthStack in communities
- Share helpful tips and tutorials
- Demonstrate how you use specific features

### 4. Track Your Progress

Check your dashboard regularly to see:
- How many clicks you're getting
- Which referrals are pending vs. successful
- How close you are to the next tier

### 5. Time Your Shares

Share during:
- Product launches or updates
- When you achieve something with SynthStack
- During relevant discussions in communities

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I refer myself?

No, self-referrals are not allowed and will not be counted.

### How long do I have to earn credit for a referral?

Referrals are attributed within a 30-day window from when someone clicks your link.

### Do my referrals need to verify their email?

Yes, referred users must verify their email address for the referral to count.

### Can I use multiple discount codes?

Generally, only one discount code can be applied per purchase. Check the specific terms of each code.

### Do credits expire?

No, credits earned through referrals do not expire.

### What if my referral asks for a refund?

If a referred user receives a refund, the referral status may be updated accordingly.

### How do I see my referral history?

Your complete referral history is available in the **History** section of your referral dashboard.

### Can I change my referral code?

No, your referral code is permanent. This ensures consistent tracking.

### What's the difference between credits and discount codes?

- **Credits**: Currency for using SynthStack features (AI generations, workflow executions)
- **Discount codes**: Percentage or fixed amount off purchases/subscriptions

### How do I contact support?

If you have issues with your referrals or rewards:
- Email: support@synthstack.app
- In-app: Use the AI Copilot for quick answers
